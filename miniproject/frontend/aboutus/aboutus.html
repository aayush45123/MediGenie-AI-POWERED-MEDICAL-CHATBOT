About Our Medical Chatbot Project

Our medical chatbot platform is designed to improve healthcare accessibility by providing reliable medical information and simplifying the appointment booking process. Our system combines AI technology with medical knowledge to offer users a seamless experience for addressing common health concerns and connecting with healthcare providers.

Intelligent Medical Assistance

Our chatbot provides responses for 20 common medical conditions and integrates with medical APIs for more specialized queries.

Easy Appointment Booking

Users can quickly book appointments with their preferred healthcare providers through our intuitive booking system.

Secure User Data

All user interactions and medical information are stored securely in our SQL database with industry-standard encryption.
